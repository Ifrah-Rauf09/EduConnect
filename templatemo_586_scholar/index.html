How EduConnect Works

1. Find Your Tutor

Search by subject, location, availability, or price. View tutor profiles with ratings, qualifications, and teaching style.

2. Schedule Sessions

Book lessons at times that work for you. Our calendar system makes scheduling easy and flexible.

3. Start Learning

Meet in-person or connect online through our virtual classroom. Pay securely after each session.

Look for tutors with verified qualifications and positive student reviews. Consider their teaching experience, hourly rate, and availability. Many tutors offer a free introductory session so you can assess if they're a good fit.

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payments are processed securely through our platform. You only pay after each completed session, and tutors receive payment within 48 hours.
